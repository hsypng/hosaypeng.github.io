One of my fav quotes is German industrial designer Dieter Ram’s design motto “Less but better”.

His philosophy of design focuses on simplicity and purity. Rams believed that the best design involves as little design as possible, emphasizing the essentials (functionality, clarity, and lasting quality) and stripping away anything extraneous.

I believe this philosophy can also be applied to life.

Own less but better things.

Own less but better clothes.

Have less but better friends.

Eat less but better food.

Consume less but better content.

Do less but better activities.
